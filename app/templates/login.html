Wanderverse FAQs

What is WanderVerse?
WanderVerse is an AI-powered travel platform that helps you plan, organize, and share your travel journeys. From turning unstructured ideas into structured itineraries to exploring recommendations, WanderVerse makes travel planning effortless.
How does the unstructured-to-structured conversion work?
Simply write or paste text (200+ words) or upload documents (e.g., travel blogs, notes, or itineraries). WanderVerse extracts the text, separates images, and provides a structured output with details like title, duration, places to visit, and travel tips.
Is WanderVerse free to use?
Yes! WanderVerse offers a free plan with advanced tools like OCR conversion, recommendations and user profile management.
Can I share my travel stories on WanderVerse?
Absolutely! You can post your travel stories, upload photos, and share recommendations with the WanderVerse community.
How does content moderation work?
WanderVerse uses AI-backed moderation to ensure all content is high-quality, reliable, and verified. This creates a safe and engaging community for all users.
Can I search for specific destinations?
Yes! Use the search bar to find specific destinations or browse through categorized recommendations from other explorers.
What file formats are supported for document uploads?
WanderVerse supports PDFs, Word documents, and plain text files for unstructured-to-structured conversion.
How do I organize my travel destinations?
You can categorize your destinations into custom lists (e.g., "Beaches" or "Mountains").
Is my data safe on WanderVerse?
Yes, your data is secure. WanderVerse uses encryption and follows strict privacy policies to protect your information.
